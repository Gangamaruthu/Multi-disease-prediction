About Multidisease Prediction

Multidisease Prediction is a healthcare application that helps users identify potential diseases related to the heart, diabetes, kidney, liver, and lungs based on their symptoms. By using machine learning algorithms and reliable medical datasets, it provides early insights to guide timely medical consultation.

  • Symptom-Based Detection: Enter symptoms to get predictions for multiple diseases.
  • Accurate Predictions: Uses medical datasets for reliable results.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy to use without medical knowledge.
  • Risk Insights: Shows likelihood of each disease.
